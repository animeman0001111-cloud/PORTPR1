Learning from the Course

A broad, hands-on journey through engineering beyond textbooks and exams.

The Engineering Exploration course gave me a broad understanding of what engineering truly involves beyond textbooks and exams. It introduced me to different engineering fields, basic design thinking, problem-solving approaches, and how engineering concepts are applied in real-world situations. Through hands-on activities, discussions, and mini-projects, I learned the importance of creativity, teamwork, communication, and logical thinking. The course also helped me gain confidence in exploring new ideas and understanding how engineers approach challenges systematically. Overall, this course helped me develop curiosity, practical thinking, and a clearer perspective on my future path in engineering.

About the Event

Engineering Exploration is a flagship course introduced by KLE Technological University to empower first-year engineering students with hands-on experience in the engineering design cycle. The course is designed and delivered by multidisciplinary faculty with a focus on problem-solving, the engineering design process, prototyping, ethics, and sustainability. Prayog Hemanth 2026 marks the celebration of a decade of student success — proudly showcasing ten years of innovation, creativity, and academic excellence.
